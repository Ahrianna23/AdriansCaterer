Catering

Our catering service has been the foundation of our business and the reason why we are expanding. Adrian’s Catering service will always cater to the needs of its clients and go above and beyond their expectations. Bringing food that is tasteful and some of the best quality you’ll ever taste, we can cater to just about any event. It’s your average catering service with our special touch!

Restaurant

Our restaurant service will soon be open on weekends where we serve up an array of delicious food items. For now, we call it the Sunday Brunch! Guest can come into our restaurant location and enjoy many food options including desserts! We encourage you to stop by to get a taste of what we can offer.
